The Brand Sprint

4-week engagement

Who it's for

Founders preparing to launch, raise, or reposition. You need more than a quick gut-check — you need to build messaging and brand strategy from the ground up, in a compressed timeline.

What problem it solves

You don't have months to figure out your brand. You need a structured, time-boxed engagement that produces real output — not a report that sits in a Google Doc.

What you get

  • 4-week engagement with one 90-minute working session per week
  • Week 1: Audit of current positioning, competitive landscape, and audience clarity
  • Week 2: Co-creation of your core brand narrative and key differentiators
  • Week 3: Messaging framework — homepage copy direction, pitch angles, and content hooks
  • Week 4: Review, refinement, and a written implementation roadmap
  • A final deliverable document with all outputs consolidated

Expected outcome

By the end of 4 weeks, you have a messaging foundation you can use across your site, investor materials, sales conversations, and content — built collaboratively, not handed down.

Boundaries

Scoped to messaging and brand strategy. Does not include design, development, or ad management. Additional sessions beyond the 4-week scope are billed separately.
